# DiscDawg Branding Profile

Reference for all customer-facing copy, marketing, and product messaging. Use this for the web landing page, app store copy, social, and future campaigns.

---

## Product

- **Name:** DiscDawg (one word; not "Disc Dawg")
- **Tagline:** *Flight data for your disc.*
- **Alternate tagline:** *Know your throw.*

**One-liner (e.g. meta description, pitch):**  
DiscDawg is a small puck that attaches to your disc. It tracks flight data—speed, hyzer, anhyzer, distance—and syncs to your phone so you can track your stats and improve your game.

---

## Value propositions

1. **Attach to any disc** — Lightweight puck fits on your disc; throw as usual.
2. **Real flight data** — Speed, release angle (hyzer/anhyzer), and distance for every throw.
3. **Syncs to your phone** — Bluetooth to the DiscDawg app; no extra gear.
4. **Track your game** — Stats over time so you can see progress and improve.

---

## Voice & tone

- **Clear and confident** — No jargon. Explain what it does in plain language.
- **Player-focused** — For people who care about their throw and want to get better.
- **Product-led** — Lead with the puck and the data; tech is the enabler, not the hero.

---

## Key messages

- "Know your throw." — Short, memorable.
- "Flight data for your disc." — What it is.
- "Track speed, angle, distance. Improve your game." — What you get.
- "Attach. Throw. Sync." — How it works.
